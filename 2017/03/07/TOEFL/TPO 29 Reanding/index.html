TPO 29 - Characteristics of Roman Pottery

do justify to 公平评判，公平对待；
be backed up by 被…支持；
photographs and drawing 照片和图画；
tough a 艰苦的，坚韧的；
very tough, although, like all pottery, it shatters [ˈʃætə(r)] if dropped on a hard surface
非常坚韧，虽然，像许多罐子一样它会破碎，如果落到坚硬的表面；

be made with carefully selected and purified clay 用精选和提纯的黏土；
eg: It is generally made with carefully selected and purified clay, worked to thin-walled and standardized shapes on a fast wheel and fired in a kiln (pottery oven) capable of ensuring a consistent finish.
work to 根据…行事,致力于；kiln n 窑； a consistent finish 一气呵成；

eg: It takes years of hard work to be an excellent interpreter.

inevitably [ɪnˈevɪtəbli] ad 必然，必定，免不了；
minor [‘maɪnər] a 较次的，轻微的；
blemish [‘blemɪʃ] n 瑕疵；
flaw [flɔ] n 瑕疵；
eg: But what strikes the eye and the touch /most immediately and most powerfully with Roman pottery is its consistent high quality.
冲击视觉和触觉（意译为：让人瞠目结舌的是）/ 最直接最有力的
stack and store 堆积和存储；
eg: they need to be convinced of its true age.
人们很难相信它们的真实年龄；
eg: As impressive as the quality [‘kwɑləti]
of Roman pottery [‘pɑtəri] is its sheer[ʃɪr] massive [‘mæsɪv] quantity.
brittle a 硬但易碎的；
pleasant a 愉快的，吸引人的；
eg: When considering quantities, we would ideally like to have some estimates for overall production from particular sites of pottery manufacture and for overall consumption at specific settlements.
当我们考虑质量时，我们会理想化的从陶器生产商的特定地点和特定居民区的整体消耗量来估计整体的产量。
eg: Unfortunately, it is in the nature of the archaeological evidence, which is almost invariable 始终如一的，永无变化的 only a sample of what once existed, that such figures will always be elusive难以琢磨的.

question the abundance of Roman Pottery
质疑罗马陶瓷的丰富度
Mediterranean 地中海；
eg: This abundance is notable in Roman settlements (especially urban sites) where the labor {that archaeologists have to put into the washing and sorting of potsherds (fragments of pottery) } constitutes a high proportion of the total work during the initial phases of excavation.
{…}此处从句用于修饰labor；
notable a 显著的；
put into 注入,投入；
wash and sort 清洗和分类；
potshred 陶瓷碎片；
a high proportion of 很高比例的；
the initial phases of excavation 初步挖掘阶段；
derive 导出，追究起源；
eg: However, there is one exceptional dump, which does represent a very large part of the site’s total history of consumption and for which an estimate of quantity has been produced.
dump 垃圾场；
exceptional a 异常的，杰出的；
a very large part of the site 大部分地点
total history of consumption 总消耗的历史；
substantial a 大量的；
eg: Around 6,000 million liters of oil were imported into the city from overseas, imports into imperial Rome were supported by the full might of the state and were therefore quite exceptional.
由国家全力支持；
noetheless n 尽管如此，但是；
cannot fail to impress 无法不让人印象深刻
lay behind sb/sth 藏在…后面；
manufacture high-quality containers 生产高质量的容器；
on delivery 交货，演讲；
far/farther/further afield 在远方；
spot/ site 地方
get yourself on a spot of bother
让自己遇到麻烦；

TPO 29 - Competition

mechanism [‘mekə.nɪzəm] n 机制，方法；
be referred to as 被称为，被当做；
sth has been long known for sb 某事为sb所熟知；
in detail 详细地；
in considerable detail 非常详细地；
in superabundant suppy 在有余供应中； scarce a 缺乏，不足，稀少；
the concern of evolutionary biology 生物进化的关注点；
inter- 在…之间，相互；
intra- 在…里，在…内；
interspecies 不同物种间；
intraspecies 同物种内；
the size of competing populations 竞争物种数量的大小；
indigenous [ɪn’dɪdʒənəs] a 本土的，土生土长的；
indigent [‘ɪndɪdʒənt] a 十分贫穷的；
competing [kəm’pitɪŋ] 竞争的；
eg: No serious competition exists when the major needed resource is in superabundant supply, as in most cases of the coexistence of herbivores (plant eaters).
正如多数情况下植食性动物能共存；
exterminate = die out = extinct (a)= eliminate 灭绝；
a large fraction of 很大一部分；
fraction n 分数，少部分，小数；
be unable to withstand competition from invading North America species 不能抵挡北美物种入侵的竞争；
withstand v 抵住，经受住；
isthmus of Panama 巴拿马地峡（连接南北美的）；
added predation 增加的捕猎行为；
close relative with same demand 有相同需求的近亲；
joint a 共同的，联合的；
eg: The evolutionary effect of competition on species has been referred to as “species selection”; however, this description is potentially misleading. Only the individuals of a species are subject to the pressures of natural selection. The effect on the well-being and existence of a species is just the result of the effects of selection on all the individuals of the species. Thus species selection is actually a result of individual selection.

density-dependent a 密度制约的；
dependent a 取决于，依赖的；

TPO 29 - The History of Waterpower

for grinding corn 用来磨玉米；
beyond its boundaries 在他的边界外；
pass across the blades 经过浆页；

take over 接管，继承；
