Words and Phrases

leave long trail behind them 在它们后面留下长长的痕迹；

tracks 轨道，轨迹； trail 轨迹；

theory [‘θiəri] 理论，原理，学说；

theorize [‘θiə.raɪz] v 建立理论；

windstorm 风暴；

wind combine with rain 风雨交加；

desert [ˈdezərt] 沙漠；

clay [kleɪ] 黏土；

dry [draɪ] 干燥的；

occasional rain 偶尔的雨；

Sentences

eg: So I’ll just touch[tʌtʃ] on an interesting subject that I think it makes an important point.

touch on 提及，说到； subject issue assignment theme

eg: We’ve been covering rocks and different types of rocks for the last several weeks.

eg: To get started(首先) I thought I’d mention(我向我提到过) something that shows how uh… as a geologist, you need to know about more than just rocks and the structure of solid matter(固体).

eg: It’s quite a mystery[‘mɪst(ə)ri] .

eg: There are no footprints(脚印), no tyre[‘taɪr] tracks(车轮轨迹) and no heavy machinery like a bulldozer[‘bʊl.doʊzər] (推土机)… uh, nothing was ever brought in(引进) to move these heavy rocks.

dozer 推土机；

eg: Most of the rocks move in the same direction as the dominant wind pattern from southwest to northeast.

eg: It’s possible that rain on the desert floor could turn to thin sheets [ʃit] of ice when temperatures drop at night.

eg: Could a piece of ice with rocks in it be pushed around by the wind?

push around 摆布，驱使；

eg: But sadly all these ideas have been eliminated [ɪˈlɪməˌnet] as possibilities.

eg: Thing is this is a protective wilderness area.

问题是这是一个受保护的原始地带；

eg: I guess that’s all for today.

那么今天讲到这吧。
