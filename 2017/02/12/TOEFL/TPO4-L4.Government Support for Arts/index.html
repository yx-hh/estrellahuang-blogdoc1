TPO4-L4.Government Support for Arts

Posted on February 12, 2017

Words and Prases

sum up some of the main points 总结一些重点；

make first attempt to sth 对，，，做出初步尝试；

federal [‘fed(ə)rəl] 联邦的；

community [kəˈmjunəti] art center 社区艺术中心；

rural [‘rʊrəl] area 乡村地区；
you name it 应有尽有；

council [‘kaʊns(ə)l] 委员会，机构；

agency 机构；

federal government 联邦政府；

corporation 公司，企业；

patron [‘peɪtrən] 赞助人；

be built with substantial financial support from corporations

acknowledge [ək’nɒlɪdʒɪŋ] 感谢，承认，告知收到；

plaque [plæk] 匾额；

eg: Many of your cultural establishments in the United States will have a plaque somewhere acknowledging the support - the money they received from whatever corporation.

do away with 废除，去掉；

get rid of 除掉，摆脱；

as far as the public goes 就大众而言；

Structure

按时间顺序，从经济大萧条期间，肯尼迪政府以及各州政府对艺术支持的methods及其primary objective，再分别谈论了politicain， public，artists对government support for arts的态度；
