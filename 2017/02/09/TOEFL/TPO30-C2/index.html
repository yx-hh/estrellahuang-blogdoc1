Words and Phrases

that’s the thing. 问题就在这。(否定回答，相当于不)

we’re getting close to the deadline. 截止期就要到了。

the theme of night in the paintings of Vincent Van Gogh

high resolution 高分辨率；wallpaper 壁纸；

Van Gogh as an artist of nocturnal[nɑk’tɜrn(ə)l] themes(夜色主题)

intense light 强烈的光；

exactly lack [læk] brightness 缺乏亮度；

a nighttime scene 一个夜间场景；

daytime/ night paintings

focus mostly on his painting Starry Night 更关注他的画作《星夜》；

part of the permanent collection 永久收藏的部分；

it’s the inspiration[.ɪnspə’reɪʃ(ə)n] for Van Gogh’s painting.

not out on loan 没有借出；

most striking shared feature 最引人瞩目的特点；

hold a rattle[‘ræt(ə)l] 拿着一个拨浪鼓；

Sentences

eg: That’s the paradox that I really like.

eg: But it’s on loan(出借) right now to a museum in Europe.

eg: I see. Well, I am strict about having students write about paintings they can observe firsthand(直接地，directly).

have sb do sth / let sb do / make sb do 让某人做某事；

be strict about doing sth 对…严格要求;坚持…；

eg: I made sure it’s not out on loan.

eg: Van Gogh copied many Millet’s compositions.

(原来有这样，毕竟Millet，附赠Millet’s starry night一张 在yale的艺术馆里面找到的，非常高清，很真实，非常喜欢，名校资源这么好，喜欢艺术的同学们，再附赠连接一个耶鲁艺术馆,网站页面也好看)

eg: Although Millet was a realist painter, and Van Gogh a post-impressionist, the two paintings still share lots of features(有很多相同的特点), not just the name.

eg: The attributes chosen to be included in a particular miniature was often meant to communicate(传达，也有交流的意思) parents’ hopes and dreams for their child.(有复合句的时候就很难听懂了)

eg: So I think you’ll learn a lot about how people viewed children at the time the miniature paintings were done.
