TPO29-R2.Competition

Posted on February 16, 2017 本文总阅读量次

Analysis for error

Q8

Paragraph 3 supports the idea that Gause’s experiments were important because they

  • A

    provided a situation in which competition could be removed from the interaction between two species

  • B

    showed that previous ideas about the extent to which competition determines the composition of a community were completely mistaken

  • C

    helped establish that competition will remove all but one species from any given ecological niche

  • D

    offered evidence that competition between species is minimal when there is an overabundance of a single food source

选错两次B,D，正确答案C

错误原因：

第一次错：省题时迫于时间压力，没有仔细看题，故将此题误判为目的题，在例子前面找答案，但是又看不懂的句子，就选了一个含有最多和原文相近内容的句子。

第二次错：知道不是目的题，但是还是看不懂文章首句和C选项的意思，故选了一个含有 a single food source的选项。

解决办法：

分析原文

To what extent [competition determines (the composition of a community and the density of particular species)] has been the source of considerable controversy. The problem is that competition ordinarily cannot be observed directly but must be inferred from the spread or increase of one species and the concurrent reduction or disappearance of another species. The Russian biologist G. F. Gause performed numerous tow-species experiments in the laboratory, in which one of the species became extinct when only a single kind of resource was available. On the basis of these experiments and of field observations, the so-called law of competitive exclusion was formulated, according to which no two species can occupy the same niche. Numerous seeming exceptions to this law have since been found, but they can usually be explained as cases in which the two species, even though competing for a major joint resource, did not really occupy exactly the same niche.

to what extent 在多大程度上；

the source of …的原因；

considerable controversy 相当多的争议；

be inferred from 由…推断；

concurrent reduction or disappearance of another species 同时减少或者消失的另一个物种；

Russian 俄罗斯人，俄罗斯的；

tow-species 两个物种的；

On the basis of these experiments and of field observations, the so-called law of competitive exclusion was formulated, according to which no two species can occupy the same niche.

law of competitive exclusion 竞争排除法则；

formulate 表达，用简洁的语言描述，用公式表达(简记为表达)；

发现分析完原文还是不知道C选项和原文有什么关联，那就看目的词provided a situation，showed，helped establish，offered evidence，由 On the basis of these experiments and of field observations, the so-called law of competitive exclusion was formulated, according to which no two species can occupy the same niche.可知是在前个实验的基础上，formulated一个law。所以是establish。

Q9

Which of the sentences below best expresses the essential information in the highlighted sentence in the passage? Incorrect choices change the meaning in important ways or leave out essential information.

  • A

    Apparent exceptions to this law usually involves cases in which two species compete for the same major resource but occupy slightly different niches.

  • B

    Although it may appear that two species always have different niches, many exceptions show that species compete with each other.

  • C

    Cases in which two species not only compete for a shared resource but also occupy similar niches are considered exceptions to this law.

  • D

    Cases in which the two species do not occupy that same niche yet still compete for the same resource are believed to be exceptions to this law.

错选两次，两次同选D。

错误原因：

D项翻译：例子：不占用相同小生态的两个物种却仍然竞争相同的资源被认为是这个法则的特例。觉得和原文翻译能对照，殊不知，seeming是表面上的意思，所以我连错两次，所以知道反向形容词有哪些是有多么的重要啊！

apparent 明显的，表面上的；

原文Numerous seeming exceptions to this law have since been found, but they can usually be explained as cases in which the two species, even though competing for a major joint resource, did not really occupy exactly the same niche.

对这个法则来说无数表面上的特例被发现，但是他们通常被解释为两个物种的例子，即使竞争一个主要的共同的资源，也不会真的占用两个相同的小生态。

Q10

According to paragraph 4, how does competition affect evolution?

  • A

    It results in the evolution of physical structures that allow the species to compete with each other more effectively.

  • B

    It results I the evolutionary extinction of all but one of the competing species.

  • C

    It results in the competing species evolving in such a way that they no longer compete for the same resources.

  • D

    It results in the competing species evolving to become so much like each other that competition between them eventually disappears.

选错两次，同选A

错误原因：

翻译A选项：生物结构进化的结果允许物种间能够更有效的竞争。

看原文

Competition among species is of considerable evolutionary importance. The physical structure of species competing for resources in the same ecological niche tends to gradually evolve in ways that allow them to occupy different niches. Competing species also tend to change their ranges so that their territories no longer overlap. The evolutionary effect of competition on species has been referred to as “species selection”; however, this description is potentially misleading. Only the individuals of a species are subject to the pressures of natural selection. The effect on the well-being and existence of a species is just the result of the effects of selection on all the individuals of the species. Thus species selection is actually a result of individual selection.

强点部分翻译：在相同生态小环境中竞争资源的物种的身体结构倾向于逐渐进化成一种方式，这就是允许它们去占领不同的小生态。竞争的物种也倾向于去改变它们的范围，为了让它们的领域不再重叠，竞争进化对物种的影响被指为物种选择。

14

C

Investigation of the ecological role of competition is difficult because ordinarily the competition cannot be observed directly and must be inferred from its presumed effects.

E

Competition between a pair of species tends to lessen over time because the species tend to evolve to occupy different ecological niches and ranges.

选C没选E

C项翻译，竞争的生态角色的研究是困难的因为通常竞争不能被直接的观察，必须从可能的影响中推断。

原文是： The problem is that competition ordinarily cannot be observed directly but must be inferred from the spread or increase of one species and the concurrent reduction or disappearance of another species.

问题是竞争通常不能直接被观察但是必须从一个物种的扩散和增加和同时一个物种的减少和消失中推测

原文和选项同义改写：原文是已知影响，推测原因；选项是竞争要从可能的影响中被推测。然而不选的原因是它是个细节。想强调的应该是生物学的那个实验，以及由实验得出的结论。

E项翻译，一对物种之间的竞争倾向于减少，随着时间的流逝，因为物种倾向于进化成占领不同的小生态和范围。

这个点事第4段的主要内容，但是我由于没看懂第四段，故没有选择此选项。算算第四段没看懂错了三题，天哪。
