TPO29-R2.Prerequisites for a film theory course

Posted on February 16, 2017 本文总阅读量次

Words and Phrases

as prerequisite [pri’rekwəzɪt] 作为先决条件；

drop back 回到原点；

drop it now 现在放下/ 放弃；

frankly 坦率地，直率地；

in-depth 彻底的，深入详尽的；

catch up 跟上，赶上；

Sentence

pretty much everyone else 差不多每个人；get 明白；

eg: It seems that pretty much everyone else in the class gets what they are supposed to be doing but I’m not so sure.

似乎班上的每个人都知道他们要做什么，但是我不确定。

eg: Well, the class is for students who are really serious about film.

这个课程是针对认真对待电影的学生而开的。

eg: Did you concentrate mainly on form or content?

你们过去主要关注形式还是内容？

eg: we have two introductory courses that are supposed to be taken before you get to my course, one in film art, techniques, technical stuff and another in film history.

get to my course 接触我的课程；

techniques 方法，技能；

technical stuff 技术细节；

eg: Students in the class (you are in) should be pretty far along in film studies.

你所在课堂的学生应该在电影研究上很有造诣了。

eg: In fact, usually the system blocks anyone (trying to sign up for a class for they shouldn’t be taking).

事实上，系统经常阻止每个不应该上这门课的学生注册这门课。

eg: For your own good, I really suggest you dropping back and starting at the usual place.

eg: Like any other professional training we build on previous knowledge.

像其他任何的专业训练一样，我们建立在前人的知识上。

eg: Maybe you can recommend some extra reading that I can do to catch up.

eg: Well, are you intending to study film as your main concentration?

eg: There is no harm in try to keep up with this class.

eg: We have them on video.

我们拥有他们的视频。

eg: It’s still a pretty tall order, and we will be moving right along, So you will really need to stay on top of it .

a tall [tɔl] order 高要求；right along 不停地，不断地；stay on top of it 坚持住；

eg: OK, I’ve been warned.

好的，我收到警告了。

eg: That would give you a better handle on the subject.

这样做，你会更好的掌握这门课。

Structure

学生提出问题，教授询问学生的学习情况，并给出建议，建议未被完全接纳，转换另一种建议，对话结束。
