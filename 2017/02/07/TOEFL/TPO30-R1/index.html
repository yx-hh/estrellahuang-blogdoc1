Words and Phrases

take the place of / substitute for/ in place of/ instead of 代替；

take place in the other circumstance 在其他的环境中发生；

developmental characteristics 发展的特点

特点：characteristic/ trait/ peculiarity[pɪˌkjuliˈerəti]

特征：feature/ characteristic/ character (特征，性格，角色)

most extensively 最广泛地；

attention-getting(引起注意力的) behavior;

maze training 迷宫训练；

enriched 丰富的；

impoverished 贫瘠的；

weight/ size 重量、大小；

synaptic[sɪ’næptɪk] connections in the brain especially the cerebellum 大脑中的突触连接特别是小脑；

sage 明智的；

Sentences

eg: Play is consists of leaping, running, climbing, throwing(扔), wrestling(摔跤), and other movements.

eg: Exactly why animals play is still a matter debated in the research literature, and the reasons may not be the same for every species that plays.

eg: Whether play occurs or not depends on the intention of the animals, and the intentions are not always clear from behaviors alone.

eg: Play is not without considerable costs to the individual animal.(并无没有)

eg: Great activities also increase the risk of injury in slipping or falling.

eg: Thus, active play may provide necessary stimulation to the growth of synaptic connections in the brain, especially the cerebellum, which is responsible for (负责)motor functioning and movements.

eg: Prey species, like young deer or goats, for example, typically play by performing(执行) sudden flight movements and turns, whereas predator species, such as cats, practice stalking(跟踪), pouncing(猛扑), and biting(咬).

eg: Play can also provide practice(实践) in social behaviors needed for courtship(求爱) and mating.

eg: They need to learn how to be dominant(主导的) and submissive(服从的) because each monkey might have to play either role in the future.

eg: There is a danger, of course, that play may be misinterpreted（曲解） or not recognized as play by others, potentially leading to aggression.

eg: Thus, many species have evolved clear signals to delineate playfulness.

eg: Dogs, for example, will wag their tails, get down their front legs, and stick their behinds in the air to indicate “what follows is just for play.”(下面是逗你玩的，不过人类应该是fun，好玩)

stick their behinds 撅起屁股；
