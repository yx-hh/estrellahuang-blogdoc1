Appreciation

eg: There’s no evidence of people ever living here.

没有人们曾近生活在这的证据。

eg: Cave bears, yes, but not humans.

有洞穴黑熊但是没有人类。

eg: And each time you’d have to bring along torches to light your way.

每次你都必须要携带火把来照亮你的道路。

eg: What inspired the paleolithic artists to make such beautiful art in such inaccessible places?

什么激励了旧石器时代的艺术家制造出如此漂亮的艺术在这样难以接近的地方？

eg: There is a significant interest in large dangerous animals.

对大型危险动物有明显的兴趣。

eg: But whatever it was, it was worth it to them to spend hours deep inside a cave with just a torch between them and utter darkness.

eg: So rock shelters, shallow caves were valued as homes protected from the wind and rain.
