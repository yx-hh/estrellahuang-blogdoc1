DECLARATION:

From today on，I will continually update this section for the English book “LEAN IN”, what I’m gonna to do is that translate some unfamiliar, lovely, dramatic or inspired phrases or sentences and share with you so that whenever you meet some new words, you don’t need to consult a dictionary but to look at Estrella’s blog.

In order to let more English learner know more about new words, In my passage when I translate them into Chinese, I would like to use more original meaning of the words rather than paraphrase, if you have any questions or funny things to communicate with me about my passage,welcome！

Today, I read the following section:

Introduction: Internalizing the Revolution(引言：深化改革 internalize：内部化)

eg: I got pregnant with my first child in the summer of 2004. (在2004年的夏天我怀上了我的第一个孩子)
eg: I was running the online sales and operations groups at Google. I had joined the company three and a half years earlier when it was an obsure start-up with a few hundred employee in a run-down office building. (run:运行，操作，管理；obsure：不为人知的；start-up：刚创办的；run-down：破败的；office building：办公大楼)
eg: By my first trimester, Google had grown into a company of thousands and moved into a multibuilding campus.(trimester:妊娠的三月期（医学上将妊娠期分为三个期，每三个月为一期，在本文中指Sheryl怀孕的头三个月)

typical morning sickness （typical:一贯的，典型的；morning sick：指孕妇的晨吐）
eg: I gained almost seventy pounds, and my feet swelled two entire shoe sizes, turning into odd-shaped lumps I could see only when they were propped up on a coffee table.
​ 我胖了大约70磅，“大约31.75公斤”，我的脚整整肿大了两个鞋码，变成了奇怪形状的肿块，我只能在他们被放在咖啡桌上的时候
name after me (以我的名字命名)

a rough morning(一个糟糕的早晨)
make an important client meeting(开一个重要的客户会议)
an ongoing problem(一个持续存在的问题)
eg: The only spot I could find was quite far away.(我能找到的唯一的地点相当的远)

eg: I sprinted across the parking lot, which in reality meant lumbering a bit more quickly than my absurdly slow pregnancy crawl.(我全速奔跑过停车场，这事实上意味着缓慢吃力的移动只是比我不合理的缓慢的孕妇爬行快一点 sprint: 全速奔跑；in reality：事实上；lumber:缓慢吃力的移动；absurdly：不合理地 pregancy：怀孕；crawl：爬 )

eg: this only made my nausea worse(这仅仅只让我的恶心加重 nausea：恶心)
pray 祈祷
a sales pitch 推销
recount these troubles to my husband (recount sth to sb 向某人叙述某事)
point out 指出
expectant mothers (预产期的母亲们 expectant：预产期的)

march in 进入（这里形容比较有气势的的进入）
waddle in 摇摇晃晃的走进
toys and gadgets 玩具和小玩意儿
strew 把..布满 all over the floor
in a yoga position in the corner 以瑜伽的姿态在一个角落
preferably sooner rather than later 最好是快点而不是以后；preferably 最好

eg: To this day , I’m embarrassed that I didn’t realize that pregnant women needed reserved parking until I experienced my own aching feet.(这一天我很羞愧我没有意识到孕妇需要专用的车位直到我经历了双脚肿痛)

most senior women 最高级别的女性

eg: it has never occured to me 我从没有想过这件事
lack the confidency or seniority to demand that the problem to be fixed.(缺乏自信或者级别去要求问题被解决 seniority 级别，资历)

eg: We stand on the shoulders of the women who came before us, women who had to fight for the rights that we now take for granted.
​ 我们站在那些站在我们之前的女性的肩膀上，他们为我们现在认为理所当然的权利而奋斗

my longtime mentor 我长期的顾问

eg: I figure I am getting the same brains for less money, Her reaction to this was to feel flattered. It was a big compliment to be told that she had the same brains as a man.
​ 我计算过了我得到相同的大脑用了更少的钱，她对此的反应是感到被奉承。这是一个很大的赞美对于被告诉她有和男性一样的大脑（此处描述男女的担任相同工作时，薪资不等的待遇）

unthinkable 难以置信的
compensation 薪酬

deny women basic civil rights 否定女性基本的公民权利
eg: Worldwide, about 4.4 million women and girls are trapped in the sex trade.
​ 全世界范围内大约有4.4亿妇女和女孩受困于性交易

eg: In places like Afghanistan and Sudan, girls receive little or no education, wives are treated as the property of their husbands, and women who are raped are routinely cast out of their homes for disgracing their families.Some rape victims are even sent to jail for committing a “moral crime”.
​ 在这样的地方比如说阿富汗和苏丹，女孩接受到很少或者没有教育，妻子被认为是丈夫财产的一部分，妇女们被强奸后通常被抛弃除家门因为为他们的家庭蒙羞了。一些强奸受害者甚至被送进监狱承认自己犯了道德罪.

eg: We are centuries ahead of the unacceptable treatment of women in these countries.
​ 我们领先那些难以被接受的对待的妇女的国家几百年。（head of 领先 unacceptable 难以接受的）

stop sb from doing sth 阻止某人做某事

eg: When the suffragettes marched in the streets, they envisioned a world where men and women would be truly equal. A century later, we are still squinting, trying to bring that vision into focus.
​ 当妇女参政论者行走在街道上，他们想象着一个世界男人和女人被平等的对待。一个世纪之后，我们任然斜视着，努力的去带来愿景被实现（suffragette 妇女参政论者； envision 想象 squint 斜视，眯着眼看；vision愿景，视觉 ；into focus 实现）

eg: the blunt truth is that men still run the world.

生硬的事实就是男人仍然在操纵着世界。blunt：生硬的，直率的

parliaments 议会，立法机构

eg: Since then, women have slowly and steadily advanced, earning more and more of college degrees, taking more of the entry-level jobs, and entering more fields previously dominated by men.

​ 从那时起，女性缓慢而平稳的前进，赢得了越来越多的学位，得到了更多入门级的工作，进入更多的领域，以前是由男人所控制的

eg: Despite those gains, the percentage of women at the top of corporate America has barely budged over the past decade.

​ 尽管这些收获，在美国公司的高层，女性的百分比几乎没有波动在过去的几十年。（percentage n 百分比； corporate a 公司的；barely ad 几乎没有；budge v 轻微移动 corporate America 美国公司）

eg: A meager twenty-one of the Fortune 500 CEOs are women.

​ 区区21个财富500强CEO 是女性。（meager a 不足的；微薄的 ）

eg: Women hold about 14 percent of executive officer position, 17 percent of board seats, and constitute 18 percent of our elected congressional officials.

​ 女性占据14%的有执行权的职位，17%的董事职位，构成了18%的我们选举的国会官员

eg: While women continue to outpace men in educational achievement, we have ceased making real progress at the top of any industry.

​ 当女性持续超越男性在教育成就上的时候，我们停止取得进步在任何顶级的企业。

eg: This means that when it comes to make decisions that most affect our world, women’s voices are not heard equally.

​ 这意味着当提到作出影响世界的决定的时候，女性的声音总是不能平等的被听到。

eg: Progress remains equally sluggish when it comes to compensation. In 1970, American women were paid 59 cents for every dollar their male counterparts made.

​ 进步仍然是相当的迟缓当提到报酬的时候，在1970年，美国妇女被支付59美分而相应职位的男性挣一美元。

​ （小编再意译一下:当提到报酬的时候进步仍然是缓慢的，在1970年，相同职位的女性与男性的工资比是0.59:1）

​ sluggish a 缓慢的，迟缓的；counterpart n 同行，职位相当的人；

eg: By 2010, women protested, fought, and worked their butts off to raise that compensation to 77 cents for one dollar men made.

​ 到2010年，女性抗议，斗争，放弃自己的目标全力以赴去提升报酬到0.77:1相比于男性的。:haha:

eg: As activist Marlo Thomas wryly joked on Equal Pay Day 2011,” Forty years and eighteen cents. A dozen eggs have gone up ten times that amount.”

​ 作为一个活动分子马洛. 托马斯冷笑2011年的同酬日说: “40年，薪酬只增加了18美分，而一打鸡蛋的价格已经长了十倍”

​ wryly ad 挖苦地，嘲讽地；equal pay day 同酬日；go up 上涨 ；amount n 数量，金额；

disheartening a 令人沮丧的； front-row a 前排的；

entry-level a 入门级的，适合初级用户的；

eg: I thought that was due to historical discrimination against women. The proverbial glass ceiling had been cracked in almost every industry, and I believed that it was a matter of time until my generation took our fair share of the leadership roles.

​ 我认为是历史性的歧视反对着女性，众所周知的天花板在每个行业被破解，我相信公平的分享领导的角色直到我这一代是时间的问题。

​ discrimination n 歧视；proverbial a 众所周知的；industry n 工业，行业；take a fair share 公平的分享；

​ fair a 合理的，恰当的；公平的；

eg: But with each passing year, fewer and fewer of my colleagues were women, more and more often I was the only wowan in the room.

​ 但是每一年过去 ，我越来越少的同事是女性，越来越频繁，我是房间里唯一的女性。
