eg: Being the sole woman has resulted in some awkward yet revealing situations.

做一个孤独的女人导致了一些尴尬的但是有启示作用的情况。

revealing a 有启示作用的,有意义的；

chief operating officer 首席运营官；

step in to complete a funding round. 介入完成一轮融资；

step in 介入，干预；

raise capital 筹集资本；

for the initial pitch to private equity firms 初次接触和游说私募公司

eg: Our first meeting was held in the kind of corporate office featured in movies, complete with a sprawling view of Manhattan.

我们的第一次会议在像电视里的那种公司办公室，可以一览无余曼哈顿的美景。

sprawling a 杂乱无章的；a sprawling view 一个庞大的视图；

feature v 以….为特色；

complete v 完成；

eg: I offered an overview of our business and answered questions.

我概括的介绍了我们公司的业务并回答了一些问题。

overview n 概述，概论；

so far so good 到目前为止一切都好；

we break for a few minute . 我们休息几分钟。

women’s restroom 女性洗手间

eg: He stared at me blankly.

他一脸茫然的看着我。

eg: My question had completely stumped him. I asked, “How long have you been in this office?” And he said ,”one year.” “Am I the only woman to have pitch a deal here in an entire year?” “I think so,” he said, adding, “or maybe you are the only one who had to use the bathroom.”

我的问题完全难住他了，我问“你在这个办公司多久了？”他说“一年。”我是唯一一个女性提出这个问题在这里的一整年里吗？”“我认为是如此的”他说，又补充道“或许你是唯一一个必须使用女性洗手间的人”!

eg: It has been more than two decades since I entered the workforce, and so much is still the same. It is time for us to face the fact that our revolution has stalled.

自从我加入劳动大军已经有超过20年了，是时候让我们面对现实了，我们的革命已经停止了。

stall v 停止，停顿；

eg: The promise of equality is not the same as true equality.

允诺平等并不等于真正的平等。

eg: A truly equal world would would be one where woman ran half our countries and companies and men ran half our homes.

eg: Legendary investor Warren Buffett has stated generously that one of the reason for his great success was that he was competing with only half of the population.

legendary a 非常著名的； investor n 投资人；population n 人口；

eg: when more people get in the race, more records will be broke.

get in 进入；race n 竞争，竞赛；

eg: The achievements will extend beyond those individuals to benefit us all.

成就将会超越个人到益于我们所有的人。

at a book party 一个签售会；

autobiography n 自传；

dictator n 独裁者；

mighty a 强大而有力的；

a somber night 一个阴暗的夜晚；somber a 阴暗的，忧郁的；

eg: A guest asked her how American women could help those who experienced the horrors and mass rapes of war in places like Liberia. Her response was four simple words: “More women in power”

horror n 恐惧； mass a 大量的；rape n 强奸，强奸案；Liberia 利比里亚；

eg: Conditions for all women will improve when there are more women in leadership roles giving strong and powerful voice to their needs and concerns.

take down the barriers that prevent more women from getting to the top

扯下障碍；（意译：拆除障碍）阻止更多的妇女进入社会的高层

eg: Women face real obstacles in the professional world, including blatant and subtle sexism, discrimination, and sexual harassment.

女性面临着真实的障碍在职场，包括公然的或隐性的性别歧视，差别待遇，性骚扰。

eg: Too few workplaces offer the flexibility and access to child care and parental leave that are necessary for pursuing a career while raising children.

workplace n 工作场所；flexibility n 弹性；parental leave 产假；

a far great extent 更大程度上

eg: Men are promoted based on potential, while women are promoted based on past accomplishment.

eg: in addition to the external barriers erected by society, women are hindered by barriers that existed within ourselves.

加上外部的障碍由社会建立，女性也被存在自己身上的障碍阻碍。

in addition to 加之，加上；erect v 建立；hinder v 阻碍；exist v 存在；

一个句子中出现了两次被barrier阻碍的表达，可以学习哦！

eg: We hold ourselves back in way both big or small, by lacking self-confidence, by not raising our hands, and by pulling back when we should be leaning in.

我们克制自己在大的或小的方面，通过缺乏自信，不举起我们的手，拉回自己在我们本该向前一小步的时候。

eg: We internalize the negative messages we get throughout our life—-the massages that say it’s wrong to be outspoken, aggressive, more powerful than men.

我们内部化（消化）我们获得的消极的信息贯穿一生，这些信息说，直言不讳的，有上进心的，比男性更加有权利是错误的。

eg: We lower our own expectations of what we can achieve.

我们降低我们能实现的期望。

majority a 大多数的；

eg: we compromise our career goals to make rooms for partners and children who may not even exist yet.

eg: this is not a list of things other women have done. I have made every mistake on this list. At times, i still do.

eg: My argument is that getting rid of these internal barriers is critical to gaining power.

get rid of 摆脱； internal barriers 内部的矛盾； gaining power 获得权力；

eg: Others have argued that women can get to the top only when the institutional barriers are gone.

其他人主张女性能够到达顶端只有当制度的障碍消失的时候。

eg: This is the ultimate chicken-and-egg situation.

这是一个终极的鸡和蛋的情况。

eg: The chicken: women will tear down the external barriers once we achieve leadership roles. We will march into our boss’s room and demand what we need, including pregnancy parking,or better yet,we’ll become bosses and make sure all women have what they need.

鸡：女性将会撕破外部的障碍一旦我们到达了领导者的角色。我们将会走进（有气势的）我们老板的办公室，要求我们所需要的，包括孕妇停车位，或甚至更好的，我们将成为老板确保所有的女性有他们所需要的。

tear down 撕下，扯下； march into 长驱直入，大踏步前进； pregnancy parking 孕妇停车位；

eg: The egg: we need to eliminate the external barriers to get women into those roles in the first time.

蛋：我们首先需要消除外部的障碍使女性进入这些角色

eliminate 消除 ；external barriers 外部障碍；

eg: Both sides are right. So rather than engage in philosophical arguments over which comes first, let’s agree to wage battles on the both front.

两边都是对的，因此与其参与哲学的讨论，谁在前，不如让我们赞同发动战役在两条战线上。

engage in 从事，参与，忙于；over who comes first 在谁先的（问题）上； wage battles 发动战争；

on the both front 在两个战线上；

eg: Internal obstacles are rarely discussed and often underplayed.

内部的障碍很少被讨论，通常被轻描淡写。

underplay 暗中活动（意译为轻描淡写）；

eg: Throughout my life, I was told over and over about inequalities in the workplace and how hard it would be to have a career and a family.

贯穿我的生活，我被一遍又一遍的告诉在工作环境中的不平等和事业与家庭间将会是如何的艰难。

inequality 不平等；

eg: I rarely heard anything, however, about the ways I might hold myself back.

但是我很少听到任何关于可能会抑制自己的方法。

held back 阻止，抑制；

eg: These internal obstacles deserves a lot more attention, in part because they are under our own control.

这些内部的障碍值得更多的关注，在某种程度上他们在我们自己的控制范围内。

deserve 值得； in part 在某种程度上；

eg: We can dismantle the hurdles [‘hɜrd(ə)l] in ourselves today. we can start this very moment.

我们今天就能拆除心中的障碍。我们能够非常快速的开始这个。

dismantle 拆开，取消； hurdle 跨栏，障碍；very moment 非常快速;

eg: The first chapter of this book lays out some of the complex challenges women face.

这本书的第一章安排了一些女性面对的复杂的挑战。

eg: Each subsequent chapter focuses on an adjustment or difference that we can make ourselves: increasing our self-confidence, getting our partners to do more at home, not holding ourselves to unattainable standards.

每个下一章专注于一个调整或者不同我们能为自己而做的：增加自信心，让我们的另一伴在家中做更多，不要抑制自己对没获得的标准。

eg: I do not pretend to have perfect solutions to these deep and complicated issues. I rely on hard data, academic research, my own observations, and lessons I have learned along the way.

我不会假装有一个完美的解决办法对那些深刻而又复杂的问题。我依赖于硬数据，学术研究，我自己的观察，以及我沿路上的教训。

feminist manifesto 女权主义宣言；

eg: It is not a feminist manifesto-okay, it is sort of a feminist manifesto, but one that I hope inspires men as much as it inspires women.

up against 面临，遭遇；

have the same objectives 有相同的目标；

live their lives as they desire 过着他们想要的生活；

eg: We each have to chart our own course and define which goal fit our lives, values, and dreams.

我们每个人都必须制定我们自己的独一无二的课程和确定哪个目标填满我们的生活，价值观和梦想。

chart course 制定课程； chart 动词 制定计划； define 明确，定义；

eg: I was also acutely aware that the vast majority of women are struggling to make ends meet and take care of their families.

我也是如此强烈的意思到绝大部分的女性在努力的使收支相抵（养家糊口），照顾他们的家庭；

acutely 强烈的，深深的感受到；the vast majority of 绝大多数的；be struggling to do sth 努力做某事；

make ends meet 使收支相抵，量入为出；

eg: Part of this book will be most relevant to women fortunate enough to have choices about how much and when and where to work; other parts apply to situations that women face in every workplace, within every community, and in every home.

这本书的部分将会是和足够幸运的女性最相关在选择多少，怎样，哪里去工作，另外一部分应用于女性情况如在每个工作地方，在每个团体，在每个家里。

caution 谨慎，告诫；

eg: I know some believe that by focusing on what women can change themselves- pressing them to lean in, it seems like I am letting my institutions off the hook.

我知道一些信仰是通过关注女性如何改变自己，逼着她们向前一小步，这好像我是为了让自己摆脱困境一样。

eg: Or even worse, they accuse me of blaming the victim.

甚至更糟糕的，他们控告我指责受害者

eg: That will resonate with women in a broad range of circumstances.

这将会与女性在广泛的范围内产生共鸣。

resonate 共鸣；

eg: My hope is that my message will be judged on its merits.

我的希望是我的信息有它的价值来判断

transcend 超越；

eg: The time is long overdue to encourage women to dream possible dream and encourage more men to support women in the workforce and in the home.

早就应该鼓励更多的女性去做可能的梦和鼓励更多的男性在劳动力和家里支持女性；

reignite the revolution 重新点燃革命

eg: The shift to a more equal world will happen person by person.

向一个更加平等的世界改变将会发生建立在人本的基础上。

shift 改变
